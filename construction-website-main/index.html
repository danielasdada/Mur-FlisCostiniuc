our services

waterproofing membrane

Installation of a high‑performance waterproof membrane beneath tiles protects bathrooms and wet areas from moisture damage, mold, and leaks.

wall tiling

Wall tiling involves the precise installation of ceramic, porcelain, or natural stone tiles to enhance the appearance and durability of indoor and outdoor walls.

floor tiling

Floor tiling provides a strong, stylish, and long-lasting finish using materials such as ceramic, porcelain, or marble, suitable for bathrooms, kitchens, and living areas.

bathroom tiling

Bathroom tiling combines waterproof materials with expert installation techniques to deliver a functional and elegant surface for walls and floors in wet areas.

kitchen tiling

Kitchen tiling enhances the hygiene and aesthetics of kitchen spaces by applying easy-to-clean, heat- and stain-resistant tiles on walls, backsplashes, and floors.

tile repair & replacement

Tile repair and replacement services address damaged or outdated tiles, restoring the look and function of floors and walls with precise matching and clean finish.

outdoor tiling

Outdoor tiling uses weather-resistant tiles to improve the durability and style of patios, balconies, and terraces, making exterior spaces both functional and elegant.
